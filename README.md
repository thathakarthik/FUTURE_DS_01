# Business Sales Performance Analytics – Task 1

## 📌 Objective
Analyze business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, and regional performance.

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel

## 📊 Key Insights
- Revenue peaked in Q4 (October–November).
- United Kingdom contributed the majority of revenue.
- Top 5 products drive most of the total sales.
- Clear seasonal sales pattern observed.
- Slight decrease in customer count in 2011 compared to 2010.

## 🚀 Recommendations
- Increase marketing campaigns before Q4 peak season.
- Focus inventory on top-selling products.
- Improve customer retention strategies.
- Expand operations in high-performing regions like the UK.

## 🏁 Conclusion
The business demonstrates strong seasonal performance with high dependency on the UK market and leading products. Strategic improvements in retention and expansion can enhance profitability.
